Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket is a mobile adaptation of the beloved Pokémon TCG, offering a streamlined and accessible experience for both newcomers and seasoned collectors. Developed by Creatures Inc. and DeNA, and published by The Pokémon Company, the game was officially released on October 30, 2024, for iOS and Android devices.

Designed with mobile play in mind, Pokémon TCG Pocket simplifies traditional TCG mechanics to create quick, engaging matches. Players construct 20-card decks, eliminating the need for energy cards, as energy is automatically provided each turn. To win, a player must defeat three of the opponent's Pokémon, reducing the typical six required in standard TCG play.

The game offers daily rewards, including two free booster packs, allowing players to gradually build their card collection. Additionally, the Wonder Pick feature enables players to select cards from booster packs opened by others, enhancing the social and collectible aspects of the game.

Pokémon TCG Pocket regularly introduces new content through themed expansions and in-game events. Notable expansions include "Genetic Apex," featuring iconic Pokémon like Charizard and Mewtwo, and "Mythical Island," which introduced Celebi EX and other powerful cards. The "Extradimensional Crisis" expansion brought Ultra Beasts into the game, adding depth and variety to gameplay strategies.

Since its release, Pokémon TCG Pocket has garnered a positive reception for its user-friendly interface and engaging gameplay. The game's accessibility makes it an excellent entry point for those new to the Pokémon TCG, while its depth and regular updates keep veteran players invested. With over 100 million downloads and more than $500 million in revenue as of early 2025, the game has solidified its place in the mobile gaming landscape.
