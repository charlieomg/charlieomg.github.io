Once Human is a free-to-play multiplayer open-world survival game set in a post-apocalyptic future. Developed and published by Starry Studio, a subsidiary of NetEase, the game was released for Windows on July 9, 2024, and for iOS and Android on April 23, 2025

In Once Human, players awaken in a desolate world ravaged by an event known as the "Starfall," where an alien substance called Stardust caused most living beings to mutate into violent creatures known as Deviations. As a Meta-Human—a biologically enhanced human capable of controlling Stardust—players embark on a journey to uncover the secrets behind the catastrophe and confront the forces responsible

Once Human offers a blend of survival and looter-shooter mechanics within a shared sandbox environment:
· Resource Gathering and Crafting: Players collect materials to craft weapons, tools, and shelter, essential for survival in the hostile world.
· Base Building: Construct and fortify bases to protect against both environmental threats and other players.
· Combat and Exploration: Engage in battles with mutated creatures and explore abandoned towns to uncover resources and lore.
· Sanity and Survival Systems: Manage hunger, hydration, and sanity meters, which affect health and performance. Prolonged exposure to supernatural areas can decrease the sanity meter, reducing the player's maximum health pool.

Unique Features
· Deviants: Capture and collect friendly Deviations to assist in combat or resource gathering.
· Eternaland: A personalized area where players can build diverse structures and territories, preserving their creations for future scenarios.
· PvP and PvE Modes: Engage in player-versus-player and player-versus-environment scenarios, each offering unique challenges and rewards.
· Custom Servers: Players can create and customize private servers with adjustable parameters such as weather patterns, day-night cycles, and resource respawn rates, allowing for tailored gameplay experiences.
