  • Why does HD sometimes look like SD?

    HD raises stream bitrate—so you see sharper motion. When a frame is static, SD and HD look similar. If the whole stream seems blurry, check your connection stability: cloud gaming needs a more stable link than YouTube.

  • I have fast internet; why do I still lag or stutter?

    Speed alone isn’t enough—low latency & steady ping matter more. Torrents, video downloads or crowded Wi-Fi can spike jitter. Switch to 5 GHz or wired, and pause any large downloads.
